Why Equipment Matters

Your scuba gear is what allows you to breathe, move, and stay safe underwater. Learning to use it correctly is one of the first steps in becoming a certified diver.

Essential Gear

  • Mask: Helps you see clearly underwater.
  • Snorkel: Allows breathing at the surface without using your tank.
  • Fins: Provide efficient movement through the water.
  • Wetsuit/Drysuit: Keeps you warm in different water temperatures.
  • Buoyancy Control Device (BCD): Lets you float, sink, or stay neutral in the water.
  • Regulator: Delivers air from your tank to your mouth.
  • Tank: Holds the compressed air you breathe.
  • Weights: Help you descend by counteracting buoyancy.

Optional Accessories

Many divers also use underwater cameras, dive computers, and lights to make their experience even better.
