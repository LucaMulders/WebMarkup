Fun Fact: The word SCUBA stands for “Self-Contained Underwater Breathing Apparatus.”

Definition

Scuba diving is the practice of exploring underwater environments while using specialized equipment that allows you to breathe beneath the surface. It offers both adventure and a unique perspective on marine life.

What You Can Experience

  • Colorful coral reefs full of life
  • Shipwrecks and underwater caves
  • Close encounters with marine animals
  • The feeling of weightlessness under the sea

Why People Dive

People scuba dive for recreation, research, photography, and even archaeology. It’s both a sport and a way to connect with nature on a deeper level.
